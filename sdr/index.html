This is a sales-first role. It is not passive support work. You are expected to actively hunt, engage, convert, and close customers across multiple channels.

Role Overview

Your job is to create demand and close

The SDR is responsible for daily selling activity from first contact to completed transaction. You will handle outreach, conversations, objections, order details, payment coordination, and follow-up opportunities.

Sales and lead generation

Actively find potential customers, perform outreach through social media, chat, calls, and other channels, and generate consistent daily leads.

Social media selling

Post product offers, promos, and content regularly while turning inquiries into paying customers.

Meetings and closing

Set up sales discussions when needed, handle objections, and close deals efficiently.

Operating Rhythm

What the day-to-day looks like

Order processing

Receive and confirm customer orders, verify customer and delivery details, and coordinate fulfillment with the Company.

Payment collection

Collect payments through approved methods, remit on time, and keep proper transaction tracking.

Follow-ups and retention

Follow up on leads who have not converted, encourage repeat purchases, and offer upsells where appropriate.

Compensation

Performance-based earnings

This is a commission-based contractor role with no fixed salary. Strong performers can earn significantly more because compensation follows actual sales results.

Important

Exclusivity for PoppersGuyPH

You will have exclusive selling rights for PoppersGuyPH, which also means you cannot sell competing or similar products while representing the brand.

This matters because customer trust, brand handling, and pricing discipline are part of the role. Applicants must be trustworthy, especially when handling payments.

Who should apply

Strong communicator

Comfortable with hard selling

Self-driven and reliable

Performance Expectations

How success will be measured

Daily activity

Consistent outreach, posting, conversations, lead generation, and follow-ups.

Sales targets

Fast response time

Growth Opportunity

Top performers can grow with the team

SDRs who prove consistent performance, clean customer handling, and strong sales discipline may be considered for full-time employment with benefits, higher commission structures, or team lead roles.

Evaluation windows
