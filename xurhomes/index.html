Scope Of Service

3.1 Cold calling and lead nurturing

The first pillar focuses on outreach. These services are designed to help Xur Homes test the market rapidly, qualify serious buyers, and move interested prospects toward trippings.

AI Caller

Best for rapid market testing and bulk filtering. JCIT can work through up to 100 leads in a single day and qualify interest using standard screening questions like salary bracket and preferred location.

Live Caller

A dedicated professional human caller assigned to your agency to handle deeper nurturing, follow-ups, and property viewing bookings.

Digital Infrastructure

3.2 Digital marketing and web development

Basic Website

A clean 4-page responsive website such as Home, About Us, Property Listings, and Contact Us. Ideal for a straightforward digital brochure.

Interactive Website

A dynamic platform with an AI chatbot for FAQs on property details, downpayment schemes, and an automated scheduling tool for tripping reservations.

Social Media Manager

End-to-end management of Facebook, TikTok, or Instagram to build trust and run organic or paid lead-generation campaigns.

Visual Direction

Digital credibility that supports lead conversion

A stronger digital footprint helps Xur Homes look more credible, answer buyer questions faster, and turn social or outbound attention into measurable next steps like inquiries and site visits.
