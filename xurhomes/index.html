Scope Of Work

The core website build

We will develop a customized, responsive four-page website designed specifically for real estate conversion. Each page has a defined role in helping Xur Homes inform, impress, and convert prospects.

About Us

A dedicated page covering the agency’s history, mission, core values, and team expertise to build trust with potential clients.

Property Gallery

A visually organized digital catalog with high-quality images, brief descriptions, and key property details for listings currently for sale.

Blog

A content hub for real estate tips, market updates, and neighborhood guides to support organic traffic growth through SEO.

Contact Us & Lead Form

A streamlined contact page with a secure inquiry form so clients can submit details and interests directly to the sales team.

Premium Package

Interactive features that keep the site working around the clock

AI Chat Bot Assistant

An automated assistant trained to answer common client questions and capture leads even outside office hours.

Interactive Live Chat

Allows agents to step in and chat with site visitors in real time, helping the team respond faster and close deals sooner.

Schedule Management System

An integrated booking calendar where clients can schedule viewings or consultations without the usual back-and-forth.

Visual Direction

Real estate presentation that feels premium from the first scroll

The site can combine elegant listing imagery, cleaner information hierarchy, and stronger calls to action so each property and service touchpoint feels more credible and easier to act on.
