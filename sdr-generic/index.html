Key Responsibilities

Own the sales motion from outreach to follow-up

The SDR will support JCIT Services customer acquisition by finding new prospects, starting conversations, qualifying interest, and helping move customers through the buying journey.

Sales and lead generation

Identify potential customers, perform daily outreach, and build a consistent pipeline of qualified leads.

Social and chat selling

Post offers or updates when needed, respond to inquiries, and guide prospects toward clear next steps.

Sales discussions

Set up conversations, explain offers clearly, handle objections, and help close sales efficiently.

Customer Handling

Keep details clean and follow-ups timely

Order coordination

Collect and confirm customer details accurately, then coordinate next steps with the appropriate internal contact.

Tracking and reporting

Maintain clear records of prospects, customer conversations, sales progress, and completed transactions.

Retention and upsell

Follow up with unconverted leads, encourage repeat purchases, and identify natural upsell opportunities.

Requirements

What we are looking for

Strong communication skills

You can write and speak clearly, respond professionally, and adapt your message to different customer types.

Comfort with direct selling

You are comfortable with outreach, follow-ups, objections, and sales targets.

Self-driven work style

You can manage your own activity, prioritize leads, and stay consistent without close supervision.

Sales experience is a plus

Experience in online selling, chat sales, outbound calls, retail, or field sales can help you ramp quickly.

Performance Expectations

How success is measured

Consistent daily activity

Regular outreach, timely replies, active prospecting, and disciplined follow-up.

Quality conversations

Clear discovery, accurate qualification, and confident objection handling.

Reliable coordination

Accurate customer details, clean handoffs, and proper tracking of every sales opportunity.

Who This Role Is For

Proactive sellers

Organized communicators
