DESIGN BUILD LAUNCH SUPPORT

JC IT Services is hiring a Web Designer and Developer to create, improve, launch, and maintain business websites for clients. This role is for someone who can combine sharp visual judgment with practical website operations.

Required Skills

Applicants must be proficient in WordPress and cPanel, including day-to-day site updates, hosting controls, domains, email setup, SSL, backups, and launch support.

Key Responsibilities

Design, build, and maintain client websites

The Web Designer and Developer will support business websites from initial layout through launch, then help keep them updated, secure, and easy for clients to use.

Website design and page builds

Create clean, responsive pages that are easy to navigate and aligned with each client's brand and goals.

WordPress administration

Manage pages, themes, plugins, forms, menus, media, updates, basic troubleshooting, and content changes.

cPanel and hosting support

Handle common hosting tasks such as domains, DNS checks, email accounts, SSL, backups, redirects, and file access.

Launch and QA

Check mobile responsiveness, links, forms, performance basics, browser behavior, and launch readiness before handoff.

Work Style

Reliable, practical, and detail-oriented

Client-ready communication

Explain website updates clearly, ask for the right details, and keep task progress visible.

Design judgment

Use layout, spacing, typography, imagery, and calls to action thoughtfully instead of just filling templates.

Ownership mindset

Notice issues, fix what you can, escalate what needs help, and protect client sites from careless changes.

Requirements

What we need from applicants

Proficient in WordPress

You can confidently work inside WordPress, update content, manage plugins, adjust themes, and troubleshoot common site issues.

Proficient in cPanel

You understand the common cPanel tools needed for website hosting, files, emails, domains, SSL, backups, and redirects.

Comfortable with HTML and CSS basics

You can make practical frontend adjustments and understand how website structure and styling work.

Portfolio or examples preferred

Send links to websites, landing pages, mockups, or maintenance work that show your taste and execution.

Nice To Have

Skills that help you stand out

Website speed and SEO basics

Familiarity with image optimization, metadata, headings, clean URLs, and basic performance checks.

Forms and integrations

Experience setting up contact forms, tracking pixels, analytics, embedded widgets, or simple automations.

Visual tools

Comfort with tools such as Figma, Canva, Photoshop, or similar design and asset-preparation workflows.
