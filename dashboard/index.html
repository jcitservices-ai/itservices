What JCIT Digital is building

This engagement covers the full design-to-deployment lifecycle for a custom Lead Tracker Dashboard. The build is structured to centralize lead activity, support daily operational review, and create a cleaner reporting workflow for the Craftsmanship Marketing team.

Application and dashboard creation

End-to-end design, development, and deployment of a bespoke high-performance Lead Tracker Dashboard.

Brand assets
